Why Adopt from Us?

Adopt-a-lotl is a heart-run business. We understand the delicacy of the axolotl in the wild, being endangered due to Mexican pollution. A portion of all profits goes directly to Axolotl care and research labs, to help clean the waters, and help return the Axolotl to their native habitat.
